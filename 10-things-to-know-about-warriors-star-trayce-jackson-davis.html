    10 things to know about Warrior's Star Trayce Jackson-Davis

    Trayce Jackson-Davis was selected by the Golden State Warriors with the 57th pick in the 2023 NBA draft. Despite being passed over by several teams, Jackson-Davis is determined to prove his worth and make an impact in the league.

    17 Feb 2024, 00:00

    1. Drafted by the Golden State Warriors

    Trayce Jackson-Davis was selected by the Golden State Warriors with the 57th pick in the 2023 NBA draft. Despite being passed over by several teams, Jackson-Davis is determined to prove his worth and make an impact in the league.

    2. Motivated by the Draft Snub

    Watching most NBA teams pass on him during the draft has fueled Jackson-Davis's motivation to succeed. He draws inspiration from Draymond Green, who was also drafted after numerous players and went on to become a key player for the Warriors.

    3. A Solid All-Around Game

    Jackson-Davis brings a versatile skill set to the Warriors. Known for his athleticism and basketball IQ, he possesses the ability to contribute in various aspects of the game. His well-rounded abilities make him a valuable asset for the team.

    4. High Energy and Determination

    One of the defining traits of Jackson-Davis's game is his high-energy approach and playing with a chip on his shoulder. He is determined to prove himself at the professional level and make an impact on the court.

    5. Impressive College Career

    Prior to joining the NBA, Jackson-Davis had a successful college career at Indiana University. He became the program's all-time leader in rebounds and blocks, showcasing his dominance in the paint.

    6. Learning from Coach Steve Kerr

    Jackson-Davis expressed excitement about joining the Warriors and learning from head coach Steve Kerr. Kerr's experience and coaching expertise will provide valuable guidance for Jackson-Davis as he transitions to the NBA.

    7. Playing alongside Star Players

    Joining the Warriors means Jackson-Davis will have the opportunity to play alongside established stars like Steph Curry and Klay Thompson. Sharing the court with these talented players will further enhance his development as a professional.

    8. The Warriors' Belief in His Skills

    The Warriors are impressed with Jackson-Davis's skills and believe he will be a valuable addition to their active roster. Their confidence in his abilities speaks volumes about his potential impact on the team.

    9. Fans Rallying Behind Him

    Despite the draft snub, Jackson-Davis's determination has resonated with the Warriors' fans. They are rallying behind him and showing their support as he embarks on his NBA journey.

    10. Building a Legacy

    Jackson-Davis has the opportunity to carve out his own legacy with the Warriors. By proving his worth and making a significant impact on the team, he can cement his place in the franchise's storied history.

    Trayce Jackson-Davis's journey from being passed over in the NBA draft to becoming a key player for the Golden State Warriors is a testament to his resilience and determination. With his versatile skill set, high energy, and the support of his team and fans, Jackson-Davis has the potential to make a significant impact in the league and leave a lasting legacy with the Warriors.
