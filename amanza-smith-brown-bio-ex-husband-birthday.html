    American model Amanza Smith Brown rose to fame being one of the casts in Netflix’s reality series Selling Sunset. Besides, she appeared in headlines for filling missing cases against her ex-husband Ralph Brown, who seemed missing in action since September 2019.

    Amanza Smith-Brown is an American model, actress, Interior designer, and real estate agent. She works for a professional real estate brokerage Oppenheim Group.

    The multi-talent personality is a married woman raising two children. What happened to Amanza’s ex-husband? Where is Ralph Brown now? Moreover, follow the article to know more about Amanza’s ex-husband, children, age, career, and net worth.

    Amanza Smith Brown’s Bio; Age, Family, Ethnicity

    The 44-year-old diva celebrates her birthday on 12 December. She was born in 1996 and hails from Indiana, United States. Born to White Mother and African Father, Amanza is of African-American ethnicity.


    However, the exact identity of her parents and siblings falls off the radar. Holding American nationality, she has a strong belief in Christianity.

    Being a model, Smith has an attractive body. Her cheek dimples add beauty to her charming face. Going through her body measurements, she is 5 feet 10 inches (1.77 m) tall in height with 58 kg body weight.

    Amanza went to a local high school and later graduated from Indiana University with a Major in Interior designing.

    Selling Sunset Stint, Job & Net Worth

    After completion of her education, Amanza moved to Los Angeles and established herself as a model.  She registered her profile with a talent management company named L.A Talent.

    Meanwhile, Smith got the fortune to be part of various magazines and TV commercials. Likewise, she was cast in two seasons of the International Gameshow Deal or No Deal. Besides, she had a brief stint as NFL cheerleader for Indiana Polis Colts.

    Later, Amanza appeared in the second and third seasons of Netflix’s reality show Selling Sunset which aired in 2020. Besides, she runs her own business and is Chief executive Officer of Amanza LLC including an online boutique, Shop Social Scenes which sells designer clothing, home decor, luggage, jewelry, and more.


    Moreover, Amanza works for a professional real estate brokerage Oppenheim Group. Her ability to openly and honestly communicate with clients has been a key to her success in the business.

    Being a successful model, entrepreneur, and real estate agent, Smith has garnered massive assets from her career stint. She now enjoys an estimated net worth of $1million.

    Who Is Amanza Smith Brown’s Ex-Husband?

    Amanza ex-husband‘s name is Ralph Brown to whom she got married in 2010 per bio. The couple had an Indianapolis nuptials. The duo co-parents two children: daughter Noah and son Baker.


    However, their wedding life did not work out leading to divorce in 2012. Ralph and Amanza have been equally sharing custody for their children. In 2014, Amanza was rumored of dating Hollywood actor Taye Diggs which did not last long.

    Being a single mother, she receives immense love and support from her children.

    Where Is Her Ex-Husband?

    Ralph has been looking after his children since his split with Amanza. According to her, Ralph is missing since September 2019 per bio.

    She was on her way to file a missing case. Later, Ralph came in contact. According to Ralph, he had struggled through depression after retirement due to this continuous pain and did not want to be in contact.
