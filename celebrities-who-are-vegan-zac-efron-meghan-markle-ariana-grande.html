    Many celebrities use their voice to raise awareness about various causes, veganism being one of the most common ones. Some feel very passionate about it: Joaquin Phoenix, for example, addressed our disconnection from the natural world in his Oscar speech and Morrissey has been vocal about animal rights ever since the eighties.

    Related: Jay-Z’s 5 Closest Friends (& 5 Celebs He Stays Away From)

    There are plenty of celebrities who follow a vegan and plant-based diet. Not all of them share their dietary preferences with the world, though. Since they are mostly known for what they do rather than what they eat, many fans never find out their favorite celebrities are vegans. Here is a list of ten celebrities who abstain from eating animal products.

    10 Zac Efron

    Following a vegan diet is just one of the many things Zac Efron does to stay in shape. He first spoke out about it two years ago, claiming it helps his sleep, metabolism, and overall well being. There's even a pre-red-carpet smoothie he shared with the world: it's made of spinach, avocado, kale, blueberries, raspberries, bananas, almond milk, chia seeds, and coconut water.

    What's confusing about Zac's vegan philosophy is that last year, he posted a photo on Instagram that shows him fishing with his friend. Not very vegan of him!

    9 Ellen Page

    A quick glance at Ellen Page's Instagram instantly reveals she's a huge animal lover. Named as the sexiest vegan in 2014 by PETA, Ellen has been practicing veganism for ages.

    The compassionate actor fights for equality on all fronts. She once famously said: “Why are vegans made fun of while the inhumane factory farming process regards animals and the natural world merely as commodities to be exploited for profit?”

    8 Woody Harrelson

    Sure Woody Harrelson seems a bit sketchy sometimes, but this outstanding actor has been a vegan for half of his life. He takes veganism a bit further than most: he only eats raw foods. It gives him much more energy than cooked and non-vegan meals. Also, it apparently slows down the aging process.

    He has turned his co-stars vegan and wrote an appeal to the Pope to go vegan for Lent. Born and raised in Texas, he feels strongly about an event called "the Bacon Bash", where wild hogs are captured and tormented for entertainment purposes.

    7 Casey Affleck

    Casey Affleck has been a vegan for 15 years. Why? His reasons mostly pertain to the inhumane meat industry. He works with PETA and has made several public service announcements with the organization.

    He is married to a fellow vegan, Summer Phoenix, Joaquin Phoenix's sister. A few years ago, she filed for divorce, but the vegan duo never finalized it. They remain married to this day.

    6 Liam Hemsworth

    Fans are swooning over Liam Hemsworth's Instagram posts that feature him with dogs and animals, but not many know that he has also actually been a vegan since 2015.

    Related: Liam Hemsworth’s 10 Closest Celebrity Friends

    His ex-wife, Miley Cyrus, is also a vegan, but she's always been far more vocal about it than Liam. Following the same diet might mean a lot in a marriage, but it certainly isn't enough to make it last.

    5 Carrie Underwood

    Most known as the winner of American Idol, Carry Underwood is all about living a healthy lifestyle. Her Instagram is filled with posts of her working out and she just recently published a bestseller called Find Your Path.

    Carrie Underwood follows a vegan diet but makes an exception with eggs here and there. In her defense, they come from her own chickens that she has in the backyard. Otherwise, she's a big fan of tofu and smoothies.

    4 RZA

    Wu-Tang clan member RZA tries his best to lead a peaceful life, vegan diet included. His transition towards vegan diet began back in 1995 when he gave up red meat. Soon, poultry and fish followed. He simply didn't want to be responsible for the deaths of any living creatures.

    RZA's exemplary lifestyle inspired his sons to be vegans as well. In 2019, he created a vegan jacket, made out of ocean plastic. He is a true environmentalist.

    3 Meghan Markle

    Ever since she started being associated with the British royal family, Meghan hasn't publicly discussed her private life, her diet included. Most tabloids cared more about the royal wedding than what she eats. It's known that she has broken her veganism on several occasions, but otherwise, the Suits star is all about living an ethical lifestyle.

    Related: 10 Ways Meghan Markle Is Like Princess Diana (& 5 Big Differences)

    Lately, the media has labeled her as a "flexitarian". Be that as it may, she has promoted a plant-based diet way before it was cool.

    2 Ariana Grande

    The press and fans are too obsessed with her dating life and hit songs to dive deep into Ariana Grande's dietary preferences. As a typical Cancer, she possesses a sensitive, emotional soul and cares deeply about fellow humans as well as animals.

    She has been vegan since 2013 and is now very active in the animal rights community. Her fans can see what she's eating on Snapchat, oatmeal with berries being one of the singer's favorite meals.

    1 Travis Barker

    Blink 182's drummer became a vegan back in 2008 after surviving a plane crash. However, he has ditched meat when he was a teenager. Just like Woody Harrelson, Barker similarly reports having loads of energy since becoming a vegan. He never gets tired, despite the fact that drumming demands quite a lot of physical energy.

    He's not the only Blink 182 member who stays away from animal products - Matt Skiba is a vegan too.
