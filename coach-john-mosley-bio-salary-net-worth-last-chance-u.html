    Coach John Mosley On Last Chance U: Basketball Season 2

    Netflix’s Last Chance U is back for season 2. Viewers were introduced to coach John Mosley in season 1 where he was working with the basketball team of East Los Angeles College. He took a struggling program and turned it into a perennial playoff team, having coached the Huskies to eight consecutive postseason appearances before this season.

    As season 2 unfolds, the coach is ready to make sure that his team reaches the top of the CCCAA. Moreover, this is also his 11th season with the ELAC staff member. His work at the college has also helped him earn the title of the 50 Most Impactful Coaches in Junior College Men’s Basketball.

    Coach John Mosley Salary

    While the average wage for a head coach in college basketball is reportedly $41,000, John Mosely earns roughly $50,000 a year as the head coach of East Los Angeles College.

    In terms of his professional career, John Mosely began in 1998 at Master’s University as an assistant men’s basketball coach. He spent eight years working at the institution, where he also taught physical education and kinesiology until 2006. He switched roles as a teacher and an assistant men’s basketball coach at California State University beginning in September 2006. John spent five years working for the institution before leaving in 2011.

    John began working for Athletes to Business in August 2011 as a career coach or advisor on professional game plans. The privately held corporation provided coaching, advice, and assistance in integrating exceptionally gifted student-athletes into the business world. Athletes were also able to get fulfilling jobs because of it.

    John left his position with the business and joined East Los Angeles College as the academy’s director. He began serving as the college’s head coach in July 2012, two years after taking the job. While starting in July 2016, he also taught kinesiology.

    In July 2021, John took to his Instagram to give a glimpse of his marriage. He shared, “20 Yrs ago on this day God gave me my wife (Marriage is The Grace Of Life). HAPPY ANNIVERSARY to the most beautiful, God-fearing Women in the world..!! @mrslmose . We are just getting started. 8-14-2001. #happyanniversary #graceoflife #familyadvocate”

    So in July 2022, the couple celebrated their 21st anniversary. On Mother’s Day 2021, he wished his wife, “Happy Mother’s Day to the most beautiful wife..!! Words can’t express it. You are #thegraceoflife.”

    Talking about his wife, LaShaunda is working at Los Angeles United School District. She worked as a 5th-grade teacher at Dyer Street Elementary in 2010. According to Cal Salaries, her yearly salary was $90,981 in 2015.

    Talking about her age, being born in 1976, she is currently 46 years old. Her maiden name is Pierce and her full name is LaShaunda Monique Pierce.

    The “power couple” reportedly shares three children: Jackson, Moriah, and Leah. Jackson is attending St. Francis High School and is expected to graduate in 2023. He is active on Twitter (@JacksonMosley4) and Instagram (@jackson.mosley). Jackson stands tall at 5’10” and plays varsity basketball.

    Moriah Mosley is attending Verdugo Hills High School where she is playing as a junior and will graduate in 2024. Leah Mosley the youngest of three graduated from middle school in June 2022 and joined Verdugo Hills High School as a freshman in the same year.
