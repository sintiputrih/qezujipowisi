    Early Life & Biography

    Cynthia Bailey was born in Decatur, Alabama, on February 19, 1967. She is the firstborn child of Barbara Bailey, who worked at a sewing factory, and Elijah Bailey, who worked at an auto factory. Her mother gave birth to her when she was only 18 years old, and a year later, she got another child named Malorie. While growing up, Cynthia said she was greatly inspired by her grandmother Mae Ford, who held several jobs at once.

    In high school, she competed in local beauty pageants, and she emerged the very first African American homecoming queen. She was a student at the University of Alabama in Huntsville, but at the age of 18, she moved to New York City to pursue a modeling career.

    Personal Life

    On July 24, 2010, Baileys got married to Peter Thomas, who is a club owner. The wedding took place at the Fernbank Museum of Natural History and was shown on The Real Housewives of Atlanta season three. Cynthia and Peters announced their separation I 2016, and in 2017, the divorce was finalized. Prior to the marriage, Cynthia dated Jayson Williams, but she turned down his proposal in 1996. She dated Leon Robinson, and they have a daughter together. She is currently dating Mike Hill, who is a sports correspondent at Fox.

    In 2011 Cynthia opened her own modeling school called Bailey Agency School of Fashion, based in Atlanta. In 2015 she appeared in the documentary Black Friday, which highlighted consumer spending within black communities. In 2019 she opened a wine bar, and the launch party was aired on The Real Housewives of Atlanta season 11.

    Career

    Cynthia began taking part in beauty pageants when she was still in high school. At the age of 18, she signed a five-year contract with a modeling agency in New York City, having moved from Alabama to pursue a career in modeling. In 1985 she appeared in a low-budget film called The Dark Power, marking her debut as an actress.

    Another role came in 1990 in the film Without You I’m Nothing, although it was not very successful. In 1993 she played the role of a model in the film For Love or Money, and it earned her some recognition.

    Her modeling career took her to Paris and Milan, where she was a runway model for a whole year. She then got back to New York City, and she modeled for Target, Maybelline, and Oil of Olay. She also got chances to model for Vanity Fair Magazine, Elle, Vogue, and the Glamour Magazines. Cynthia had the opportunity of appearing on the Essence Magazine twice; 1995 and 1997.

    In June 2010, Cynthia became a cast member of the reality shows The Real Housewives of Atlanta. The show mainly featured her relationship with her then-husband Peter Thomas as well as her friendship with NeNe Leaks, a fellow cast member.
