    EarthGang Biography and Wiki

    EarthGang is an American hip hop duo from Atlanta, Georgia, in the United States, composed of Atlanta-based rappers Olu (aka Johny Venus) and WowGr8 (aka Doctor Dot). They are co-founders of the musical collective Spillage Village, with JID, Hollywood JB, JordxnBryant, 6LACK, and Mereba.

    Formed in 2008, EarthGang unleashed their first EP, The Better Party, in 2010. This was followed by various singles and two mixtapes, Mad Men and Good News in 2011. In 2013, the gang unleashed their debut album Shallow Graves For Toys and was largely well-received, with Noisey calling it ”one of the most well-thought-out releases of the year” and praising the album’s ”playful delivery and biting lyricism.”

    Their sophomore album, Strays with Rabies, was unleashed to positive reviews in 2015. After signing to J. Cole’s Dreamville  Records, EarthGang unleashed a trilogy of EPs: Rags, Robots, and Royalty, leading up to their major-label debut album, Mirrorland.

    EarthGang Mirrorland

    Mirrorland is the major-label debut studio album by American hip hop duo EarthGang, unleashed on September 6th, 2019 by Dreamville, and Spillage Village. The album includes features from Young Thug, T-Pain, Kehlani, Arin Ray, and Malik. The production of the album was handled by a variety of producers including Olu, J Cole, Elite, Ron Gilmore, Christo, Blink, DJ Dahi, Childish Major and Groove, among others.
