    Pepe Diaz

    Mexican actress, Eiza and Brazillian businessman Pepe Diaz dated for a couple of years in 2011. The former lovers were open about their relationship and often gushed their affection via social media.

Finally, however, they admitted that it was Eiza’s voice.

    Actress Eiza and producer Calvin Harris sparked relationship rumors after being spotted in 2016. Them walking arm in arm fueled that fire.

    While talking about romance with musician, actress Eiza also sparked dating rumors with the Colombian singer, Maluma. The relationship assumptions started after Eiza’s supposed partner, Maluma, joined her at a nightclub in LA.

    As it was put by the close friend, the former pair are pretty flirtatious and weren’t serious as they did not believe their romance would last. However, he also added other casualties like distance, strong personalities that prevented Eiza and Maluma from committing.

    Josh Duhamel

    Amongst the dates and dating rumors, Eiza also dated Unsolved star Josh Duhamel. According to US Weekly, the former couple first met at a party after Jennifer Lopez‘s pre-Super Bowl show in MN.

    Both Eiza and Josh reportedly drank and partied together until late hours, and Josh reached out to a mutual friend for Eiza’s number. Moreover, they were face timing and texting nonstop. Josh even admitted that he’d never met anyone like her before.

    However, they parted ways in July 2018 because of busy schedules.

    Cristiano Ronaldo

    In July 2016, after Gonzalez and famous soccer star Cristiano Ronaldo was spotted together in Ibiza, people pondered if they were dating.

    A grainy picture of them surfaced online, which sparked romantic hearsay between the two celebrities. Furthermore, Eiza was allegedly joined by the suave Portuguese footballer at a Hollywood Warwick nightclub party the same month.

    However, the supposed pair parted the venue separately, and they never addressed the speculation. Cristiano is with his long-time girlfriend, Spanish model Georgina Rodriguez, and enjoying fatherhood.

    On the other hand, Eiza Gonzalez is still sparking romance rumors & searching for Mr. Perfect.

    Well, Eiza and Timothee’s fans were pretty ecstatic about the surprise couple in 2020, but their romance was short-lived. Their affair lasted as long as their 5-day Mexican vacation. Hence, their summer romance did not even make it to fall, let alone mere mention of Gonzalez during the winter interview with GQ.

    Later, Eiza and Dusty were seen on multiple outings together, and she confirmed her romance when the pair were seen hand-in-hand during a shopping trip in LA.
