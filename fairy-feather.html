    Fairy Feather (Japanese: ようせいのハネ Fairy Feather) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IX that boosts the power of Fairy-type moves.

    In the core series games

    Price

    GamesCostSell price
    SVN/A$750

    Effect

    Boosts the power of the holder's Fairy-type moves by 20%.

    Description

    GamesDescription
    SVAn item to be held by a Pokémon. This feather, which gleams faintly when hit by light, boosts the power of the holder's Fairy-type moves.

    Acquisition

    Trivia

    • The Fairy Feather is the last non-consumable type-enhancing item to be introduced that is not related to a specific Pokémon, such as Plates.
      • Prior to the introduction of the Fairy Feather in The Teal Mask, the Fairy Gem and Pixie Plate were introduced as held items that boost the power of Fairy-type attacks, but only the latter has ever been made obtainable.
