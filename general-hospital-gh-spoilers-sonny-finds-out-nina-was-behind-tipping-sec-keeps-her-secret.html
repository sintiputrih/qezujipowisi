    General Hospital (GH) spoilers hint that the plot could thicken regarding Nina Reeves (Cynthia Watros) tipping the SEC about Drew Cain (Cameron Mathison) and Carly Spencer (Laura Wright). Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) is very eager to learn about who ratted them out, and there’s a good chance he’ll get to the bottom of things. But what happens next if and when he does?

    General Hospital Spoilers – Sonny Corinthos Isn’t The Same Sonny Corinthos

    GH spoilers tease that Sonny isn’t the same old Sonny he used to be. After all, fans can attest to how much he’s changed and mellowed out since he had amnesia and fell in love with Nina in Nixon Falls. Sonny has a soft spot for Reeves, and despite the “mistakes” she’s made in the past, he’s always willing to erase them so that the two can move forward.

    If Sonny figures out Nina was behind the SEC tip, he’ll be upset. But will it be a deal-breaker in their relationship? Especially considering all the bad blood between her and Carly, and what Ms. Spencer has done to Reeves?

    General Hospital Updates – Sonny Corinthos Makes A Promise To Keep The Information Hidden

    Nina found out about the insider trading information from overhearing a conversation. It’s not like the mob boss confided in her and she blabbed. In addition, he held that “secret” from her, and she wasn’t upset at him; she didn’t retaliate against Corinthos or even make it an issue in their romance. At the end of the day, Sonny will be mad and frustrated at Nina for what she did, but he won’t necessarily break up with her. He may even try and help hide the fact that Reeves was behind it all as it would cause more drama than good at this point.

    What will Sonny do if he learns Nina was the one who tipped the authorities about Carly and Drew? Will he break up with her or just “hash it out” and return to normal?
