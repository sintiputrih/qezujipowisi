    The force is officially awake! Harrison Ford used to work as a carpenter before he landed his now famous role as Star Wars’ Han Solo, and he was supersexy.

    “Before Han Solo, there was a great carpenter named Harrison Ford,” Brazilian musician Sérgio Mendes wrote alongside a shirtless photo of a then 28-year-old Ford on Facebook on December 23. “Here he is, with his crew, the day he finished building my recording studio back in 1970.”

    “Thank you Harrison … may the force be with you …” Mendes, 74, added, gifting the Internet with the vintage snap.

    The now 73-year-old star, who reprised his role as Han in the record-breaking Star Wars: The Force Awakens, explained during a Reddit chat last year that he landed the iconic role while working as a carpenter.

    “I had helped George Lucas audition other actors for the principle parts, and with no expectation or indication that I might be considered for the part of Han, I was quite surprised when I was offered the part. I'd invest money in tools but wouldn't have money for materials, so I realized this was another way of putting food on the table. And allowing me to pick and choose from the acting jobs that were being offered at the time.”
