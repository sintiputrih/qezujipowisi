    20 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Heidi Klum has officially rebounded.

    In an interview on Katie Wednesday, the 39-year-old supermodel confirmed that she is indeed dating her longtime bodyguard Martin Kirsten, telling Katie Couric that the romance "just started."

    When Couric asked if things have progressed to a "proper relationship," Klum demurred: "I don't even know if I can call it that. I don't know."

    Kirsten became a point of contention between Klum and her future ex-husband Seal, who lashed out about the relationship in an interview with TMZ. (Seal later clarified that he never meant to imply that his wife cheated with Kirsten during their marriage; the couple announced their separation last January after seven years of marriage.)

    Continued Klum of her new love interest: "He's been with our family for the last four years . . . He's cared for our entire family, mostly for our four children, helped us tremendously." Klum and singer Seal, 49, share kids Leni, 8, Henry, 6, Johan, 5, and Lou, 2.

    "I trust him with my children's life," the German stunner continued. "He's a great man . . . and we just got to know each other from a completely different side.

    Despite the tension with Seal, "He's moved on and so have I," Klum explained.
