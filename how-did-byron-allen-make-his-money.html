The case reached the Supreme Court, with Allen arguing that racial bias played a role in Comcast’s decision. Although the Supreme Court ruled in favor of Comcast, this legal battle highlighted the challenges faced by minority-owned media companies in gaining equitable treatment.

    In addition to Comcast, Allen has also filed lawsuits against other major companies, including McDonald’s. In his lawsuit against the fast-food giant, he alleged that the company had discriminatory practices in allocating advertising spending.
