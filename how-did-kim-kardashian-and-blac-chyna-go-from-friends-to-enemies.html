    8 Blac Chyna And Kim Kardashian Used To Be Good Friends

    If you watch older episodes of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, you may be shocked to see Blac Chyna pop up on your screen. Blac Chyna and Kim Kardashian were actually friends all the way back in 2013 - before Kim's marriage to Kanye and Blac Chyna's tumultuous relationship with Rob Kardashian. They could be spotted on each other's social media, working out, and even getting their brows done together.

    6 Tyga And Kylie Got Together In July 2014

    After Chyna brought Tyga to then-bestie Kim's wedding in May 2014, Tyga was featured on Kylie Jenner's Instagram in July 2014. Tyga then announced the end of his engagement to Blac Chyna. Kyga was Instagram-official by October 2014. Blac Chyna accused Kylie of stealing Tyga from her, which likely put a strain on her friendship with Kim Kardashian.

    5 Rob Kardashian And Blac Chyna Started Dating In January 2016

    Kim Kardashian's brother, Rob, started dating his little sister's boyfriend's baby mama in January 2016. Within a few months, Rob and Blac Chyna were reportedly engaged and expecting. Their daughter, Dream, was born in November 2016. However, despite the fast timeline of their relationship, the couple's relationship was nowhere near perfect. They broke up multiple times over the course of their relationship, and their arguments were often explosive.

    4 Rob Kardashian And Blac Chyna's Show Aired In September 2016

    The ups and downs of Rob and Chyna's relationship were put on full display on the couple's reality show Rob & Chyna. The show aired until December 2016 and was even renewed for a second season. On the show, the couple fought over virtually everything from french fries to family. The series also showcased Rob's fitness journey and Chyna's pregnancy.

    3 Blac Chyna And Rob Kardashian Broke Up In Mid-2017

    In 2017, Blac Chyna and Rob Kardashian went their separate ways for the last time. After their relationship ended, Rob filed a lawsuit accusing Blac Chyna of assaulting him on December 14, 2016. He alleged that Chyna attacked him at Kylie Jenner's home while they were celebrating the renewal of their reality show. Since there was now legal trouble between the families, whatever friendship Kim had with Chyna was probably gone. Rob didn't drop the suit until this year.

    2 Blac Chyna Also Sued Rob Kardashian

    Blac Chyna then sued Rob for posting her nude pictures on Instagram after their break-up. Last month, Blac Chyna chose to settle with Rob instead of taking him to court. Though the amount for the settlement is unknown, it is good that exes are no longer fighting each other in court and can hopefully focus on co-parenting their daughter instead.

    1 Blac Chyna Recently Faced Ex-Friend Kim Kardashian In Court

    Kim Kardashian clearly places her family above any friendship. Kim recently faced Blac Chyna in court, along with Kris Jenner, Kylie Jenner, and Khloe Kardashian. Blac Chyna was finally able to take the Kar-Jenners to court after suing them for defamation in 2017. Chyna accused the family of telling E! producers that she abused Rob Kardashian to get Rob & Chyna canceled. The jury sided with the Kar-Jenners, but Chyna plans to appeal the verdict.
