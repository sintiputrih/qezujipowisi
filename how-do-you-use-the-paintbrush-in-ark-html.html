    Answer

    The Paintbrush is a decorative tool that enables for the painting of structures with dyes using a variety of different techniques. The Paintbrush may be used to paint your placed structures by putting the dye to the Paintbrush, equipping it, and then using it on the structures that you choose.

    So, how do you go about painting stuff in Ark?

    Drag the dye over the thing you wish to colour, such as armour, swords, or saddles, to see how it changes the colour. Items that can be coloured will be highlighted in green in your inventory as you drag the dye over the page. Following that, a menu will appear, from which you can pick the part of the object that you wish to paint; each location needs a different colour dye.
