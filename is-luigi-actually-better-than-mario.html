    Many fans believe that Luigi is actually better than Mario in some aspects. While Mario may be more popular and have more charisma, Luigi is loved for his dependability, underdog status, and even his mustache. He may not have the same level of charisma as Mario, but Luigi is an excellent sidekick who does everything Mario does without seeking the glory. So in some ways, Luigi can be considered better than Mario.

    Who is smarter, Luigi or Mario?

    Luigi is considered smarter than Mario. He is known for his geographical knowledge and his success in saving Mario and learning about the world in the game “Mario is Missing!”

    Who is more skilled, Mario or Luigi?

    While Mario is a capable hero, Luigi is considered to be more skilled. Luigi has a wider skill set and a unique personality that allows him to shine in different ways.

    Why do people like Luigi more than Mario?

    Luigi is often liked more than Mario because he has a more fleshed-out personality and is seen as an underdog compared to Mario. Some people also believe that Mario sometimes treats Luigi in a rude manner. Luigi’s more relatable and relatable personality makes him more likable to many fans.

    Can Mario defeat Luigi?

    Mario would hardly be able to defeat Luigi as Luigi is considered to be superior to him in every way.

    Who is Luigi’s worst enemy?

    Luigi’s arch-rival is Waluigi. He often accompanies Wario in spin-offs from the main Mario series and is known for causing mischief and problems.

    What can Luigi do that Mario can’t?

    Luigi can make use of invisibility, walk through gates and walls, and generally avoid obstacles to nab Power Stars. These are abilities that Mario does not possess.

    Does Luigi run faster than Mario?

    No, Luigi does not run faster than Mario. In fact, the longer Luigi keeps running, the worse his traction gets, creating the illusion that he’s running faster when he’s actually slightly slower than Mario.

    What is the strongest version of Luigi?

    Cape Luigi is considered to be the strongest version of Luigi. With a Cape Feather, Luigi can fly and is much stronger than his normal self.

    How much older is Mario than Luigi?

    Mario and Luigi are fraternal twins, born on the same day. However, due to the stork dropping Luigi off later in Yoshi’s Island, Mario is technically a few minutes older than Luigi.

    What is Luigi’s weakness?

    Luigi’s primary weaknesses are his poor range and poor air speed.

    Who is the second most popular Mario character?

    Luigi is considered the second most popular Mario character after Mario himself. Luigi has a significant fan base and is recognized by many as a beloved character in the Mario franchise.

    How powerful is Luigi?

    Luigi possesses various powers, with jumping being his most iconic power. He can jump 35 feet into the air and even bests Mario’s jumping distance. At his best, Luigi can jump 85 feet into the air, outperforming Mario’s jumping capabilities.

    What is Luigi’s IQ?

    Luigi’s IQ has never been officially stated by Nintendo, so it is not possible to accurately calculate Luigi’s IQ.

    Who does Luigi love the most?

    Luigi has a close and loving relationship with Princess Daisy. The two characters are often depicted together in the Mario franchise and are considered to care deeply for each other.

    Who can beat Luigi?

    Luigi can be beaten by characters like Fox, who can use his down B to send Luigi far away, or Marth and Falco, who have longer range attacks that outmatch Luigi’s.

    Who is Luigi’s evil?

    Luigi’s arch-rival and supposed evil is Waluigi. He is often seen with Wario and causes mischief and problems for Luigi and Mario.

    What is Luigi’s full name?

    Luigi’s full name is Luigi Mario. He is the younger twin brother of Mario, and their last name is Mario.

    Is Luigi skinnier than Mario?

    Luigi is often depicted as taller and thinner than Mario. He has a wider range of emotions and is seen as having more personality than Mario.

    What is the easiest Mario game to beat?

    The original Super Mario Bros. is often considered the easiest Mario game for new players. It has simple mechanics and a classic platforming style.

    What does Mario think about Luigi?

    Mario genuinely loves Luigi with every fiber of his being. He often leaves Luigi out of adventures to protect his younger brother from harm.

    What do people think of Luigi?

    Luigi is often seen as the most interesting main character in the Mario franchise. He shows a wide range of emotions and has a unique personality. Many fans appreciate his brotherly dynamic with Mario and find him more likable than Mario.

    What is Luigi’s worst fear?

    Luigi’s biggest fear is ghosts, which he often encounters and has to fight in the Luigi’s Mansion games. While he may appear scared, Luigi is not a coward but rather a scaredy-cat.

    What are Mario’s weaknesses?

    Mario’s overall range is considered below average, which makes it difficult for him to compete against characters with long and disjointed range attacks.

    Is Luigi a hero or villain?

    Luigi is considered a hero in the Mario franchise. While he may often live in his brother’s shadow and be unappreciated, Luigi is just as heroic as Mario and is always willing to help on their adventures.
