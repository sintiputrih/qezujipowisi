    Jennifer Lopez recently wore a cozy fall outfit that just so happened to perfectly coordinate with her signature nude lip.

    Yesterday afternoon, the multi-hyphenate was spotted running errands in Los Angeles, where she made a stylish appearance dressed in a beige monochromatic look. This consisted of a clingy turtleneck top in a camel color from Intimissimi, the lingerie brand for which Lopez is a global brand ambassador. For her bottoms, she wore creamy soft cargo pants with a wide-leg fit, a high-rise silhouette, and plenty of boxy pockets. And for some finishing touches, she added a pair of tan boots with a thick block heel, a pale brown Hermès Birkin bag, tortoiseshell cat-eye sunglasses, and gold hoop earrings.

    All in all, the look meshed well with her lipstick color of choice: nude, of course.

    thecelebrityfinder/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Earlier this year, Lopez spoke with Bazaar about becoming a brand ambassador for Intimissimi, which has previously worked with other A-listers like Irina Shayk and Sarah Jessica Parker.

    “I feel connected to Intimissimi,” the Hustlers actor said at the time. “They encourage women to feel confident in their skin and, more importantly, to embrace that feeling! The designs reflect that. All of their pieces are elegant, luxe, and wearable. You feel inspired and empowered as soon as you slip it on.”

    She described the Intimissimi woman as “confident, playful, strong, and timeless,” adding, “I’m proud to be an Intimissimi woman and honored that they view me as someone who emulates their own qualities of confident, lively, and strong.”
