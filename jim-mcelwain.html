    Biography

    Jim McElwain was born in Montana on March 1, 1962. Head football coach for the University of Florida Gators beginning in 2015 who previously served as the head coach at Colorado State from 2012 to 2014, helping turn the Rams from a 4-8 ball club to a 10-2 ball club and earning Mountain West Conference Coach of the Year honors. He was an offensive coordinator and QB coach at the University of Alabama under head coach Nick Saban, helping the Crimson Tide win two national titles while he was there between 2008 and 2011. He played quarterback for Eastern Washington University from 1980 to 1983.
