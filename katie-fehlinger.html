    Katie Fehlinger- Biography

    Katie Fehlinger is a renowned American television personality best known for her work as a meteorologist. She is well-known for her regular appearances on CBS3 KYW-TV in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, as well as its associated television station WPSG-TV – also known as The CW Philly 57. Katie was also well-known for her earlier appearances on Fox News and Fox Business, as well as her work for broadcasting firms such as ABC, ESPN, and WCBS-TV.

    Katie Fehlinger- Professional Career

    Fehlinger’s professional career began in the early 2000s, when she joined the RCN 4 station in Bath, Pennsylvania, and began her journey into the world of broadcasting. During her early years, she also refined her professional talents by periodically contributing to the Emmy Award-winning news and magazine show “Tempo!” which aired on WLVT-TV, a Public Broadcasting Service member station.

    Katie moved her professional broadcasting career focus to meteorology and weather casting in 2006. She covered the tornadoes that year for Fox News, covering the whole mid-western region of the United States. She also contributes to Fox Business and Sports Network programs. Throughout the following few years, Fehlinger honed her weather casting skills by working with CBS New York (commonly referred to as WCBS-TV), where she appeared in morning newscasts and weekend forecasting shows.

    Katie had previously worked for numerous different TV stations, including ABC and ESPN, as well as the Big Ten Network, Yes TV, and NESN, before joining CBS3 in September 2011, where she is still a meteorologist. Katie can also be seen on WPSG-TV as a member of The CW Philly’s morning news program, in addition to her daily on-camera appearances on CBS3.
