    Kendra Wilkinson is taking aim at Holly Madison in season 5 of Kendra on Top. In a newly released trailer, which Wilkinson shared on Twitter on Friday, March 11, the reality star blasts her former costar’s tell-all memoir, Down the Rabbit Hole: Curious Adventures and Cautionary Tales of a Former Playboy Bunny.

    “What Holly wrote in that book is pure bulls‑‑t,” Wilkinson, 30, yells in the intense clip.

    The memoir, which was published in June 2015, is filled with shocking stories about the behind-the-scenes of the Playboy Mansion, where the two women spent years as Hugh Hefner’s live-in girlfriends, as chronicled in the reality series The Girls Next Door.

    https://twitter.com/KendraWilkinson/status/708420331753451520

    In the tell-all book, Madison, 36, wrote that she hated living in the legendary mansion and called Wilkinson the “fakest person.”

    “I just think it’s up to people what they believe,” Wilkinson told Us Weekly in August 2015. “The proof is out there on The Girls Next Door. At the end of the day, people made up their minds already if they believe it or not, so I can’t dictate. I can’t manipulate anybody’s minds.”
