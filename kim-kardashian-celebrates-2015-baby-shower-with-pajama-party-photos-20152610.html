    So much shower power! A very pregnant Kim Kardashian, who’s expecting her second child (a baby boy!) with hubby Kanye West, celebrated her baby shower this past Sunday, Oct. 25, with a star-studded pajama party attended by her famous sisters, Khloe, Kourtney, Kylie, and Kendall, and other celebrity guests.

    For the occasion, the gorgeous guests wore seersucker striped pajamas alternating in periwinkle blue and pale pink colors. At one point, Kardashian’s baby bump adorably peeked out of her pajama top.

    The bash itself was hosted at the Beverly Hills home of Kim’s longtime friend Shelli Azoff (the wife of mogul Irving Azoff). It’s also the same location where Khloe’s 2009 wedding to Lamar Odom was held. A source tells Us Weekly that brunch was served outdoors under white umbrellas. Guests noshed on breakfast-friendly items like egg dishes and fresh fruit.

    Khloe, who took a break from looking after Lamar Odom at Cedars-Sinai Hospital, made a brief appearance at her sister’s shower (fresh new lob included!) The insider tells Us that Khloe headed back to Odom long before the shower wrapped up.

    A photo from inside the party showed all five of Kris Jenner‘s girls gathering around Kim on a couch. “Sister Sunday,” Khloe, 31, and Kendall, 19, both captioned their respective snaps.

    Other guests included Caitlyn Jenner, Kyle Richards, Maria Menounos, Rachel Roy, and Cara Delevingne.
