    It's going to be epic…

      You can always count on Lady Gaga to take a kooky makeup look and somehow make it look incredible – so we can't wait to see what the singer comes up with when she launches her much-anticipated beauty range later this year (if the rumours are true, that is). That's right, Instagram investigators Trendmood have done some digging, and revealed on Thursday that Gaga will be launching her very own line of products – Haus Beauty. And, according to Allure, the United States Patent and Trademark Office has confirmed the news – since the songstress' company, Ate My Heart Inc., applied to trademark the brand name in February.

      Gaga is known for her incredible outfits and stage makeup

      As usual, the standout songstress isn't doing things by halves – the range reportedly features over 70 product types, including foundations, eyeliners, shadows, bronzers, highlighters and lipsticks, as well as skincare staples such as moisturisers, scrubs, and even massage oils. Ooh-er. One thing's for sure – if Gaga knows anything, it's how to rock a smokey eye, so we're expecting great things.

      MORE: Lady Gaga's make-up artist reveals her 'sacred' creative process

      Of course, this wouldn't be her first foray into the beauty business. She has worked with MAC Cosmetics' Viva Glam campaign on two occasions to raise money for AIDS research with a limited-edition lipstick, while her signature fragrance, Fame, is a huge best-seller – despite the fact its scent is based on blood and semen. Hmm.
