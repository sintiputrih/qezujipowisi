    I have two personalities during fall. I’m either an autumn girlie who’s wearing knee-high boots, a cashmere turtleneck dress, and carrying a PSL (obvi), or I’m a couch potato who’s curled up under a blanket watching Gilmore Girls. There’s no in between — nor would I want there to be. But the latter deserves a perfectly curated ensemble as much as the first does. That’s why loungewear sets are essential to making the most out of your fall wardrobe. 

    But I get it, you may not want to drop tons of cash on a set you’re inevitably going to be sleeping in, maybe baking in, and definitely living in. That’s why I scoured Amazon to find the best $50-and-under two-piece loungewear sets that are all discounted ahead of Black Friday. Keep scrolling to discover sets that include comfy waffle-knit material, cute shorts, cozy sweatshirts, and more.

    Best Amazon Loungewear Set Deals

    Lillusory Slouchy Sweatsuit

    It’s only right for me to start by calling out Amazon’s number one best-selling sweatsuit. The boho-inspired Lillusory set features a slouchy fit, unbuttoned deep V-neck, side slits, and wide-leg pants. In other words, it’s one of those outfits that lets you move freely while still looking totally chic. One shopper claimed it’s “perfect” for fall and suggested wearing a T-shirt underneath with thick socks to be “toasty warm in front of the fire” during colder months. That sounds like absolute heaven.     

    Ekouaer 4-Piece Set

    As if things couldn’t get any better, I found a four-piece Ekouaer set that’s on sale for $32, which comes out to just $8 apiece. (Yep, you read that right.) Each set comes with pants, shorts, a one-shoulder top, and a cropped tank in colors like sage, brown, red, and more. But it’s the lightweight and breathable material that really makes this set stand out. “I am a huge fan of this lounge set,” said an Amazon shopper. “Honestly, the best thing about this set is that it's really comfortable. The material is this nice, soft stretchy stuff that I love to put on.” Wear the Ekouaer set around the house, on a plane, to bed, and just about anywhere else. 

    Automet Sweat Shorts Set

    We all know that one person who wears shorts no matter the season. Maybe it’s your best friend, or maybe it’s you. There’s no judgment here, and Automet’s sweat short set is the ideal solution. Made with thick, fleece-lined fabric, the sweatshirt has casual drop shoulders while the shorts boast a drawstring waist and tie-front closure. “This is a fabulous loungewear set,” said a reviewer. “The quality is outstanding and extremely comfortable!” And dare I say that this is one of the most versatile sets available; it mimics athleisure, allowing you to wear it at home with fuzzy slippers or out and about with sneakers and a fanny pack. You can even separate the two pieces, crafting countless ensembles.  

    Those are just a few of my favorite on-sale loungesets available on Amazon. There are tons of other options just waiting to be discovered, so take a look below for further comfy and cozy picks, starting at $23.
