    Alana "Honey Boo Boo" Thompson hasn't had an easy life. Years after finding fame on the TLC reality series Toddlers and Tiaras and then Here Comes Honey Boo Boo, she opened up about being a child star and admitted that it has been super tough. Alana's fights with her mother June "Mama June" Shannon have been highly publicized as the two continue to appear on various reality shows. Since Alana and Mama June argue so much, fans wonder if Alana has a good bond with her sisters.

    Since Alana became successful at such a young age, people are curious about how Alana spends her net worth. Her financial situation was in the news in the spring of 2024 when fans learned that Mama June stole money from Alana Thompson. According to TV Shows Ace, Mama June will give Alana the money she took from her bank account, which is reportedly $33,000 or $35,000.

    In this article, we'll take a look at Mama June and Alana Thompson's relationship after this intense situation, along with why Mama June took her daughter's reality TV earnings.

    What Is Mama June And Alana Thompson's Relationship Like Today?

    Thanks to Mama June and Alana Thompson's huge fight about Alana's reality TV earnings that were in a Coogan bank account, the mother/daughter have a difficult relationship today. According to Entertainment Tonight, Alana wants some distance from her mom, and she would rather that her mom doesn't see her at Regis University, where she is going to school.

    Alana said in an episode of Mama June: Family Crisis:

    "I'm not going to open my front door...When I moved to Colorado, I'm moving for a reason. I'm moving away -- away from the drama, away from Mama."

    Since Alana spent so much time with her mom filming their various reality TV shows over the years, and of course lived with her, it definitely sounds like she wanted some independence. It's pretty harsh for a young adult to say that they don't want their parent to see them at college.

    According to City National Bank, a Coogan bank account is when a guardian or parent of a child star puts their earnings into a bank account so they can access the money when they are of legal adult age.

    Why Did Mama June Steal Money From Alana Thompson?

    Mama June said that she spent Alana Thompson's money and purchased their home. According to People, she said in an episode of Mama June: Family Crisis:

    "It's gone! It went to the house that we bought! I spent Honey Boo Boo and the rest of the Dancing with the Stars money! It's gone! There is no money!"

    Mama June said that she made sure that her kids didn't have access to their money because she didn't want them to spend it quickly. She said:

    "Truth to be known, Pumpkin may be mad and I'm sorry and the world may be mad and think that I'm the worst person ever. I've lied to my children over the years. I've lied to them and told them that you can't get your money until you're 21 so they wouldn't blow through it."

    However, while it seems clear that Mama June spent the money that her daughter earned from her reality TV days, Mama June did say quite a few times on Mama June: Family Crisis that there was still some money in the bank account.
