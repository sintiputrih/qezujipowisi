    TikTok star known for sharing her experiences running an iconic hot dog restaurant. She and her sister became famous for running their family business Cupid's Hot Dogs in San Fernando Valley. She is known for making hot dog deliveries on classic roller skates. She has amassed more than 320,000 followers on her zengazenga account.

    Before Fame

    As a child, she helped her dad with deliveries. She was a dumpster diver.

    Trivia

    She skated at Los Angeles Fashion Week in 2022. Her TikToks feature tracks like Donna Summer's "Bad Girls" and Stevie Wonder's "Superstition."

    Family Life

    Her sister is named Kelly Walsh. The hot dog van was started by her grandparents Richard and Bernice Walsh. Her father Rick suddenly passed away in January 2009.

    Associated With

    In August 2022, she read Mary Shelley's Frankenstein.
