    Navarone Anthony Garibaldi is an American musican. He is the stepson of Legendary musician Elvis Presley. Besides his stepfather's fame, Garibaldi is the lead vocalist of the LA-based rock'n'roll band THEM GUNS.

    The Californian singer Garibaldi was born in Santa Monica on March 1, 1987. This article aims to provide a bit of an insight into the singer's life.

    Navarone Anthony Garibaldi Is Married To Elisa Achilli

    Sadly for all the female fans of the singer, Navarone Garibaldi is taken. The Close your Eyes singer married Elisa Achilli at Schloss Hunigen Hotel in Switzerland on February 15, 2022.

    He kept Achilli waiting for a long time before he put the wedding ring on her finger. They were engaged in December 2020. Garibaldi took to Instagram to announce his marriage. He posted pictures of the wedding with the caption:

    Most amazing wedding thank you for everything baby I love you.. and thank you to all bridesmaids for doing sooo much to help! And everyone for coming. #bestwedding

    Achilli also had only good things to say about her husband. She said:

    Navarone is truly the most genuine, sweet and sensitive man I have ever known and I'm so lucky that after four years of long-distance, we can start our life together as husband and wife.

    So What Is The Net Worth of Garibaldi?

    Navarone Garibaldi has yet to experience fame and success to the same level as some famous musicians like DJ Khaled. But it doesn't mean he hasn't seen any success in the field. He is now making a living doing what he loves. His estimated net worth is around $500 thousand.

    His band THEM GUNS is responsible for most of his monetary success. He has made money through streams, live performances, and merchandise sales.

    About Garibaldi's Mother Priscilla Presley

    Priscilla Presley is an American actress from Brooklyn, New York. Though she doesn't have a lot of movies under her belt, she is a well-known figure in Hollywood, thanks to her marriage to Elvis Presley.

    She is famous for The Naked Gun movies. She no longer works in film and has shifted focus to Business instead. But she did work in TV movies in 2019 with Wedding at Graceland and Christmas at Graceland.

    Besides acting, she has also studied Karate. She studied Karate with the legendary Chuck Norris.

    About Garibaldi's Father Marco Garibaldi

    Marco Antonio Garibaldi is an entrepreneur. The American originally hails from São Paulo, Brazil. He was born on November 30, 1954.

    Though Marco prefers living a quiet life, he once loved working in the limelight. Before entrepreneurship, he tried his hands on writing and directing. 

    He is a writer of the TV show Dallas and The Hitchhiker. Very Little knows he also directed the popular kid's show Mighty Morphin Power Rangers.

    Why Garibaldi's Parents Never Married?

    Marco Garibaldi and Priscilla Presley were together for a long time but, surprisingly, never exchanged wedding vows. Long after the death of Elvis, Priscilla found herself in the arms of Marco. Looking at the amount of time these two spend together, it is interesting to learn that they never got married. 

    They dated for two decades, first meeting each other in 1984 and going separate ways in 2006. Their son Navarone was born around three years into their relationship in 1987.

    Little Bit About Garibaldi's Stepfather Elvis Presley

    Elvis Aaron Presley is one of the most celebrated musicians of all time. The King of Rock and Roll is known for his timeless music, which brought him a considerable fanbase from both the old and new generations. 

    One of the most influential figures in the music industry and outside, Elvis won the prestigious Grammy Awards three times. He was also honored with the Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2018. His influence is unmatched, with the singer selling over 146 million albums.

    His death has been mourned the world over. The Can't Help Falling in Love singer tragically passed away on August 16, 1977, at 42. The reports revealed the cause of his death was a heart attack.

    How Tall Is Garibaldi? Also Know His Weight:

    Everybody Bleeds singer Garibaldi has got the looks. His mother, Priscilla Presley, was known for her appearance back in the day. You can tell the man got all the good genes from his mother.

    The American singer Garibaldi has the same height as actor Neil Hopkins at 6 Feet 1 Inch (185 cm). His size is complimented by his slim body, which weighs around 168 lbs (78 kg). 

    He has both his ears pierced and is always seen wearing earrings. He also loves to experiment with his hair. He is often changing his hair with funky colors.

    A Brief On His Band Them Guns

    Everybody Bleeds singer Navarone Garibaldi has his rock'n'roll band called THEM GUNS. The band was established in 2013 and had been going strong since.

    THEM GUNS came into existence through the band's frontman, Garibaldi and Kyle Hammod, who plays the keyboard in the band. Kyle also plays the drums. The bass player Chuck Holiday was the latest addition to the band.

    They are still trying to make a massive name in the industry, but it doesn't mean they haven't garnered some fans in their journey. The band might not have a large fanbase, but its fans are known to be loyal and will most likely stick with the band through thick and thin.

    Working With John Tuosto

    John Tuosto is a  New York drummer currently living in Los Angeles. He is a member of the band Shadow Cliq. THEM GUNS has brought him to perform with their band.

    Some have speculated that Tuosto left his old band and is now serving as the fourth member of Garibaldi's band. Although, there is no confirmation from either party on whether the speculation is true.

    He is living his best life performing with THEM GUNS. He has been making music with them and performing on stage with Garibaldi.

    Loves Spending Time On The Animal Farm
