    Wiki/Biography

    Ray Stevenson was born as George Raymond Stevenson on Monday, 25 May 1964 (age 58 years; as of 2022) in Lisburn, Northern Ireland. His zodiac sign is Gemini. He attended the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School, United Kingdom to study acting.

    Family

    Parents & Siblings

    Ray’s father was a pilot in the Royal Air Force. He has two brothers, one younger and one elder.

    In 1997, Ray got married to actress Ruth Gemmell; however, they parted ways in 2005.

    Career

    Film

    Ray started his acting career in 1998 with the British comedy-drama film The Theory of Flight in which he played the role of Gigolo.

    Ray Stevenson in the film ‘The Theory of Flight’

    Later, he appeared in many popular Hollywood films including Punisher: War Zone (2008), Kill the Irishman (2011), The Three Musketeers (2011), Big Game (2014), and Final Score (2018).

    Ray Stevenson in the movie ‘Big Game’

    Television

    Ray started his career in television in 1993 with the television series A Woman’s Guide to Adultery in which he played the role of a journalist.

    Ray Stevenson in the film ‘A Woman’s Guide to Adultery

    He was featured in various other television series including Some Kind of Life (1995), Peak Practice (1997), Red Cap (2003), Rome (2005-2007), and Black Sails (2016-2017).

    Ray Stevenson in the television series ‘Rome’

    Stage Shows

    He also participated in many stage shows like York Mystery Plays (2000), Mouth to Mouth (2001), and The Duchess of Malfi (2003).

    Award

    In 2013, he was nominated for Saturn Award for best guest performance in the television series Dexter (2006).

    • Ray moved to England from Lisburn with his family at a young age.
