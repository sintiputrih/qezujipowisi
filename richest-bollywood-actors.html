    Bollywood celebrities have always been the favorite topic not just for Indians but the world at large. The inseparable alliance of songs and Indian movies is by no means an argument but definitely something that a lot of people wonder about. You may not like it as much but Indians love musicals. In fact, an average Indian movie has about 4-7 songs. Any attempt to reduce or eliminate songs from movies has seen a corresponding drop in viewership. Yeah, that’s how much they love Musicals.

    However, that is not up for discussion today. Let’s take a look at some of the most successful actors and actresses in the Bollywood industry.

    1. Shah Rukh Kha – $800 Million

    Forbes richest Bollywood actor for 2022 is Shah Rukh Khan also known as initialism SRK. The “king of romance” as he is fondly called was born on November 2, 1965. SRK is an actor, film producer, television personality, and also businessman. He is not only the richest actor in India but also in the world.

    Shah Rukh Khan has received several awards, including fifteen Filmfare Awards, seventeen Screen Awards, thirteen Zee Cine Awards, and six IIFA Awards. His estimated net worth is $800 million.

    The Richest Bollywood Actor 2022

    2. Amitabh Bachchan – $500 Million

    Amitabh Bachchan is the 2nd richest Bollywood actor in 2022, with an estimated net worth of $500 million.  The veteran actor who started his career in 1970 is also a producer, singer, and ex-politician.

    Besides his humility and persona which exemplifies him, his legend is an inevitable part of the Bollywood industry.

    3. Akshay Kumar – $ 340 Million

    With an estimated net worth of $340 million, Akshay Kumar takes the Number 3 spot on the list of Forbes’ top 10 richest Bollywood actors in 2022. Although he started his career in 1991, he became popular following his role as Khiladi Kumar in the suspense thriller Khiladi (1992).

    The Bollywood star who is also a martial artist is often called “Indian Jackie Chan” because of the fascinating stunts he often performs in movies.

    Kumar’s first mainstream success came with the action thriller Khiladi (1992) barely a year after he began his career (1991).

    4. Aamir Khan – $ 250 Million

    Another stellar character on the Forbes top 10 richest Bollywood actors in 2022 is Aamir Khan, the “perfectionist of Bollywood”. He is a talented actor-producer; he is one of the biggest film stars in Bollywood and a director.

    With a career spanning over 30 years, Khan has amassed an estimated net worth of $250 million, making him one of the richest, most popular, and most influential actors in Indian cinema.

    Recently, he announced his decision to quit film, because he realized he wasn’t there for his family.

    Aamir Khan started his career as a child actor in his uncle Nasir Hussain’s film Yaadon Ki Baaraat (1973).

    5. Aishwarya Ria – $100 Million

    Ria is a very skilled and talented actress, she was the winner of miss world’s 1994 pageant, and she was born in 1973. Aishwarya Ria is paid nothing less than $3.2 million per movie, which makes her not only one of the highest-paid Bollywood actresses but also the richest Bollywood actress.

    The richest Bollywood actress, Aishwarya Ria charges nothing less than $3.2 million per movie, which makes her one of the highest-paid actresses in Bollywood.

    6. Priyanka Chopra – $50 Million

    Priyanka Chopra is the second richest actress in Bollywood and the 6th on the Forbes list of richest Bollywood actors for the year 2022.

    Born in 1982 in India, Priyanka is a scriptwriter, a renowned Bollywood actress, and an established entrepreneur. As a model, she won the Miss World 2000 pageant.

    She has an estimated net worth of $50 million and she charges around $1.5 to $2.7 million dollars per movie, making her one of the highest-paid Bollywood actresses.

    Chopra is one of India’s highest-paid actresses

    7. Deepika Padukone – $40 Million

    Deepika Padukone is not only one of the richest Bollywood actors but also one of the highest-paid actresses in the Bollywood movie industry.

    She is one of India’s most popular personalities, and Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2018. Recently, she receive the TIME 100 Impact Awards honor.

    They say make-up enhances beauty but this is not so for Deepika; her beauty enhances make-up. On top, she’s a very talented actress, producer, and scriptwriter. Her net worth is estimated at $40 million.

    8. Anushka Sharma – $ 36 Million

    The 8th person of Forbes’s richest Bollywood actors in 2022 is Anushka Sharma. The Indian actress and producer is also the co-founder of the production company, clean state film.

    Besides acting, Anushka makes a fortune from brand endorsements and personal investments. Her estimated net worth is $36 million.

    According to Wikipedia, she has appeared in Forbes India’s Celebrity 100 since 2012 and was featured by Forbes Asia in their 30 Under 30 list of 2018.

    9. Ranveer Singh – $35 Million

    One of India’s highest-paid actors, Ranveer Singh also made the 9th position on Forbes Richest Bollywood Actors 2022.

    He has reportedly been deemed a self-made Bollywood actor who has no connection. His career in the film industry began in 2010 and delivered a number of hit films to the industry.

    Ranveer Singh has been featured in Forbes India’s Celebrity 100 list since 2012.

    10. Kareena Kapoor – $16 Million

    Last but not least on the Forbes list of richest Bollywood actors in 2022 is Kareen Kapoor, born in 1980 to  Bollywood actors, Randhir Kapoor and Babita.

    She is very talented and unique. Speak of a remarkable actress with a glam quotient, killer cheekbones, unashamed, and willingness to accept her mistakes, Kareena Kapoor is very quick to come to mind.
