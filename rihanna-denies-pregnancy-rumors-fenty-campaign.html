    Rihanna Had No Baby Bump In The Fenty Photos

    In a series of photos shared on the Instagram account for her line, the entertainer posed in a stunning black sheer lingerie set that highlighted her incredible figure. Enjoying a sunlit summer day surrounded by lush palm trees and vibrant flowers, Rihanna showcased a plunging black bra paired with high-waisted underwear.

    The ensemble was accentuated by a sheer-lined skirt that allowed her body tattoos to be prominently displayed, adding a touch of elegance and sensuality to the look.

    Rihanna’s look was completed with a selection of sparkling accessories, including two necklaces, several rings, and bracelets that enhanced her outfit.

    In one of the images, she was seen seated on a white chair, giving a detailed view of her stylish black pumps. Her hair and makeup were flawlessly styled, reflecting her signature glamorous aesthetic as she posed gracefully for the camera.

    The post's caption read, “The original Rihanna X Adam Selman collab for Savage X is BACK,” which sparked a wave of excitement among fans. The announcement marks the return of the celebrated collaboration between Rihanna and fashion designer Adam Selman, who have previously worked together to create iconic fashion pieces.

    Why Some Fans Thought Rihanna Hid A Baby Bump

    Close

    In recent months, there have been widespread rumors suggesting that Rihanna might be expecting her third child. This includes last month, after Riri was seen by paparazzi in an oversized coat despite the warm weather, leading some to question if she was hiding a baby bump.

    The 36-year-old singer stepped out in NYC in a light grey tank top that featured a lacy bra peeking through, paired with sleek black pants. However, it was Rihanna's choice of outerwear that truly caught the eye of many. Despite the warm weather, she donned a heavy winter coat and strategically held a Louis Vuitton purse in front of her midsection.

    Her striking combination of summer attire and winter wear prompted a wave of speculation on social media, particularly on X, formerly known as Twitter.
