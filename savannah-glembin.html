    Who is TikTok Star Savannah Glembin?

    Savannah Glembin (born on April 3, 1997, age: 26 years) is a TikTok star and a USMC Veteran who is now in the news because she posted a video that people reported as child abuse.

    Glembin shared the clip on TikTok, where her husband Hank can be seen wrapping the kid in Saran Wrap; Savannah said they used the wrap to discipline their toddler, who was grumpy all day.

    The couple thought it was a funny act, but those who watched the video considered Hank and Savannah’s action aggressive. CPS (Child Protection Services) acted quickly and took custody of the kid, following which Mrs. Glembin uploaded a 3-minute video where she apologized for her actions.

    Later CPS gave clearance to the couple and sent Gunnar back home. Lady confirmed the baby’s return by sharing a video on her TikTok account; she accepted the mistake she and her husband made, promising they would never repeat it.

    Savannah Glembin’s Husband & Son

    Savannah is married to her high school darling Hank Glembin, who you all saw in the viral Saran Wrap video. According to sources, the Arizona native married Hank in 2017, after which they served together as Marines.

    In 2021 god blessed the couple with a cute little boy Gunnar, who became the center of controversy. As we told earlier, Savannah and Hank mistreated their child, after which the Police and CPS had to take the child into safe custody.

    Protection Services observed the couple closely and told them how wrong they did, but eventually, the little guy was returned.

    From Where is Savannah Glembin?

    On April 3, 1997, Savannah Glembin (age 26) was born in Phoenix, Arizona, America. Growing up as a Texan, Sav enjoyed a wonderful childhood. In high school, she developed a love for photography and began working as a freelancer. In 2015 she moved to San Diego, California, and began working as a photographer and videographer.

    In 2018 she joined the United States Marine Corps and assumed the post of Lance Corporal. In a video released by DVIDS (Defence Visual Information Distribution Service), she explained why she joined the military. FYI, Glembin served as an Aviation Ordnanceman, which means she had to work with the weapon systems of Hueys and Cobras.

    From USMC to Social Media

    After retiring from USMC, Glembin focused on becoming a social media influencer and created a TikTok account where she posted her video on December 12, 2019. Fortunately, she received a good response from the viewers, so she kept making more such videos. She even got a nose job to improve her looks and shared her experience with her fans.

    Over the years, Savannah gained immense popularity, and currently, more than 1.1 million follow her on TikTok. She also has a significant fanbase on YouTube, but her Instagram account (@savannahglembinbackup) is private.
