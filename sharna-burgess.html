    Professional Life

    Professionally, Sharna Burgess is a Ballroom dancer and reality television personality.     Dancing with the Stars

    Likewise, firstly she made television appearances on the Australian Dancing with the Stars and So You Think You Can Dance in Belgium and the Netherlands. Likewise, they were also eliminated during week 9 (the semi-finals) and ended in 5th place despite raves from the judges and high scores throughout the season.

For season 23, she partnered with race car driver James Hinchcliffe. In between the journey, she also suffered a knee injury. However, she made it to the final and became second.
