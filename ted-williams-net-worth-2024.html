    Ted Williams’ Career Earnings

During his time with the Boston Red Sox, Williams earned a reputation as one of the best hitters of all time. However, salaries in his era were a fraction of what they are today. Despite this, Williams managed to earn a considerable amount through his baseball salary, which contributed to his net worth.
