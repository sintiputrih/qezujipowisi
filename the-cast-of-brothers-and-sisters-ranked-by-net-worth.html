    Brothers & Sisters was a popular family drama series that ran on the ABC network from 2006 to 2011. It told the story of a mother and her adult children and all the trials and tribulations the family went through. From marriages to babies and more, there was never a dull moment with the Walkers.

    The series had a stellar cast of stars that featured Sally Field, Calista Flockhart, Rob Lowe, and Emily VanCamp. With such a big-name cast, fans might wonder how they all rank up when it comes to how much each of them is worth. Let's take a deep dive into the net worths of the cast of Brothers & Sisters and find out who exactly is worth the most these days and who isn't quite as wealthy.

    10 Dave Annable Is Worth $1.5 Million

    Dave Annable appears to have the smallest net worth out of the entire cast of Brothers & Sisters, but money isn't what makes this guy happy. For fans who follow him on Instagram, he never shies away from how much he loves being a family man. He and his wife decided to pack up and move out of Los Angeles during the pandemic in 2020 and bought a house in Austin, Texas. Lately the actor has been working on his recurring roles on Walker and Yellowstone.

    9 Ron Rifkin Is Worth $6 Million

    Ron Rifkin is mostly known for his role on Alias as Arvin Sloane. It's also worth mentioning that several of his Brothers & Sisters co-stars also had roles on that show. He is also known for his roles in projects such as L.A. Confidential and Boiler Room. The actor has 125 acting credits on IMDb, which is no small feat. The actor has been working onscreen since the 1960s when he appeared on an episode of Gidget.

    8 Emily VanCamp Is Worth $8 Million

    Emily VanCamp has had a very successful career as an actress. From her role on Everwood to Brothers & Sisters to Captain America: The Winter Soldier, to Revenge to The Resident, VanCamp didn't stop working until she gave birth to her first child in 2021. The fact that she has managed to find series regular roles on not one but four successful drama series says a lot, not to mention her role in the Marvel franchise.

    7 Rachel Griffiths Is Worth $8 Million

    Rachel Griffiths is most known for her role on the successful drama series Six Feet Under. She is also known for her roles in projects such as Muriel's Wedding and The Rookie. Lately she has been working on television series in Australia, where she is from. She has 63 acting credits under her name on IMDb and it is worth noting that she had small roles in hit films such as Step Up and Saving Mr. Banks.

    6 Matthew Rhys Is Worth $12 Million

    Although Matthew Rhys played a gay character on Brothers & Sisters, he is straight in real life and his married to Kerri Russel, whom he met while filming the television series The Americans. Rhys won an Emmy Award in 2018 for his work on The Americans, which can sometimes lead to higher pay for actors. He has recently gone on to star in the HBO Max series, Perry Mason. Rhys has done a bunch of voiceover work in recent years and also had a role in the film A Beautiful Day in the Neighborhood, about Mr. Rogers.

    5 Patricia Wettig Is Worth $30 Million

    Patricia Wettig is married to producer Ken Olin and they've been married since 1982. Olin worked on This Is Us, while Wettig is known for her acting work in projects such as City Slickers, Thirtysomething, and Alias. Wettig also had a major recurring role on the hit show Prison Break. Olin shared the screen with his wife in a lot of those projects, including Alias, Thirtysomething as well as Brothers & Sisters. The two purchased a home in Venice, California for $4.5 million in 2019.

    4 Calista Flockhart Is Worth $30 Million

    Calista Flockhart became a huge star thanks to her comedy-drama series, Ally McBeal. It is also worth noting that she has been in a romantic relationship with Harrison Ford for over 20 years now. The pair have been married since 2010. Along with her role on the popular legal drama, Flockhart is known for her role in The Birdcage alongside Robin Williams as well as for her recurring role on The CW drama series Supergirl.

    3 Sally Field Is Worth $55 Million

    It's no secret that Sally Field has had an extraordinary career that began when she was quite young. It's no surprise that she has a high net worth to show for it. Field is mostly known for her roles in films such as Forrest Gump, Steel Magnolias, and Norma Raye. She was also a series regular on the series The Flying Nun in the late '60s. Field won two Oscars over the course of her career, which of course led to higher pay for the actress.

    2 Rob Lowe Has A Net Worth Of $60 Million

    Rob Lowe reportedly has a new worth of $60 million. The actor has had a stellar career over the years and he began work in entertainment at a young age. He reportedly dropped $47 million on three mansions in California in 2021. He has over one hundred onscreen acting credits under his belt and has produced nineteen projects, as well. He is known for various television shows such as Parks and Recreation, 9-1-1: Lone Star, and Brothers & Sisters. Lowe is also known for his work in films such as St. Elmo's Fire and The Outsiders.

    1 Balthazar Getty Is Worth $200 Million

    Balthazar Getty's great-grandfather was J. Paul Getty and the founder of Getty Oil and had the title of one of the richest men in the world. If you are surprised by Balthazar's net worth, that should help explain it. He is a member of the Getty family and is also an actor and a musician, as he is a member of the indie rock/electronic band called Ringside. He is also a producer for a rap duo named The Wow. His acting debut was in the 1990 film Lord of the Flies and he has had recurring roles on both Alias and Charmed.
