    How did TobyMac start his Professional Career?

    TobyMac started his professional music career by forming the Christian rock group “DC Talk” with Michael Tait, who was subsequently joined by Kevin Max Smith.

    The group has so far put out five studio albums, all of which have won several awards. The entire band announced their dissolution to take a sabbatical and go solo around the year 2000.

    With the release of his first studio album, “Momentum,” which debuted at the top of the charts, TobyMac’s solo career got underway.

    His third studio album, “Portable Sounds,” debuted at the top of the US Top Catalog Albums list and the US Christian Albums chart. “Light of Christmas,” TobyMac’s most recent CD, has songs by many musicians. 2018 saw the recent release of a single by TobyMac titled “I Just Need U.”

    TobyMac has garnered and been nominated for a sizable amount of awards. According to the Grammy Awards website, he has received over 16 Grammy nominations and has won three Grammys for best contemporary Christian music albums for “Eye on It,” “This Is Not a Test,” and best rock gospel album for “Alive and Transported.”

    The American Music Award for Favorite Contemporary Inspirational Artist went to TobyMac in 2012. He won the Top Christian Artist and Top Christian Album Billboard Music Awards for his song “Eye on It.”

    In addition, TobyMac has won multiple GMA Dove Awards, including Producer of the Year, Rap/Hip Hop Album of the Year, and Rock Recorded Song of the Year.
