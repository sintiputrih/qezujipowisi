    FaZe Clan, a prominent esports organization, has garnered widespread recognition in the competitive gaming world, primarily known for its success in games like Call of Duty, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and Fortnite. Over the years, FaZe Clan has expanded into various gaming titles, amassing an impressive roster of talented individuals from across the globe.

    The Most Controversial FaZe Clan Members

  • Jarvis Kaye (Jarvis)

    • Banned from playing Fortnite for using cheats like aimbots, and was later involved in the SaveTheKids Crypto Scam, leading to his suspension from the clan.

  • Turner Tenney (Tfue)

    • Filed a lawsuit against FaZe Clan, alleging exploitation and a contract that took up to 80% of his earnings. He faced bans from Twitch and Epic Games due to his use of racial and homophobic slurs.

  • FaZe Cented

    • Removed from the team after using racial slurs and epithets during a stream, despite his notable achievements in Fortnite tournaments.

  • Ricky Banks (Banks)

    • Accused of assaulting multiple women, promoting illegal gambling websites, and using racial slurs on multiple occasions.

  • Daniel Walsh (Dubs)

    • Suspended from playing Fortnite after using a racial slur during a live stream and facing allegations of colluding to gain an unfair advantage in a tournament, resulting in a 60-day suspension.

    • FaZe Clan's Legal Disputes and Allegations

    • Tfue's Lawsuit: Filed a lawsuit against FaZe Clan, contesting an "oppressive" contract and making allegations of being pressured into drinking underage and performing dangerous stunts.
    • FaZe Barker Allegations: FaZe Clan ended its relationship with Barker, a former editor, following serious allegations of predatory and aggressive behavior by multiple women.
    • FaZe Dubs Incidents: Dubs faced a suspension for using a racial slur and was accused of cheating during a Fortnite Solo Cash Cup, leading to further controversy.

    FaZe Clan's Rise and Mixed Lows

    FaZe Clan's Business Ventures and Financial Challenges

    • FaZe Clan's collaborations with various brands and organizations, including partnerships with Gamma Labs' G Fuel, Champion, Nissan, and McDonald's.
