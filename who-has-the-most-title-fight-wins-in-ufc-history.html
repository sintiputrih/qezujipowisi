    Who has the most title fight wins in UFC history?

    27 Feb 2024, 00:00

    In the world of mixed martial arts (MMA), the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) stands as the premier organization, showcasing the best fighters from around the globe.

    One of the most prestigious achievements a fighter can attain is securing multiple title fight wins, solidifying their place in the annals of UFC history.

    Jon Jones: Setting the Record at 15 Title Fight Wins

    Jon Jones, widely regarded as one of the greatest fighters in UFC history, leads the list with an astounding 15 title fight wins.

    His dominance in the light heavyweight division was unparalleled as he secured the championship in March 2011 and went on to defend the belt eight consecutive times.

    After a brief hiatus, Jones made a triumphant return to the UFC heavyweight division, submitting Ciryl Gane in under three minutes in March 2023 to become the new undisputed heavyweight champion.

    With the potential to extend this record, Jones continues to be a formidable force in the UFC.

    Georges St-Pierre: A Welterweight Legend with 13 Title Fight Wins

    Georges St-Pierre, known for his impeccable skills in the welterweight division, achieved an impressive 13 title fight wins from November 2006 to November 2017

    . During his illustrious career, "Rush" captured both the welterweight and middleweight titles, showcasing his versatility and dominance in multiple weight classes.

    St-Pierre's remarkable reign included victories over formidable opponents such as Matt Hughes, BJ Penn, and Jake Shields, cementing his status as a UFC legend.

    Demetrious Johnson: Flyweight Great with 12 Title Fight Wins

    Demetrious Johnson, renowned as the greatest flyweight champion in UFC history, secured 12 title fight wins in the 125-pound weight class.

    His reign as the division's champion saw him fend off all challengers, displaying unparalleled skill and resilience.

    Johnson's remarkable list of victories includes triumphs over John Dodson, Joseph Benavidez, and Henry Cejudo, further solidifying his legacy as a dominant force in the flyweight division.

    Anderson Silva and Amanda Nunes: Notable Fighters with 11 Title Fight Wins

    Anderson Silva, a true icon in MMA, amassed 11 title fight wins during his legendary career. His remarkable accomplishments include an unprecedented 16-fight win streak in the UFC, showcasing his unparalleled striking prowess and defensive skills.

    Similarly, Amanda Nunes, often regarded as the greatest female fighter of all time, achieved 11 title fight wins, displaying her dominance across multiple weight classes and earning her status as a perennial favorite in UFC odds.

     

    The UFC's history is replete with fighters who have etched their names in the record books through their exceptional performances in title fights.

    Jon Jones, Georges St-Pierre, Demetrious Johnson, Anderson Silva, and Amanda Nunes stand as prime examples of athletes who have reached remarkable heights in the octagon, securing numerous title fight wins and leaving an indelible mark on the sport of MMA.
